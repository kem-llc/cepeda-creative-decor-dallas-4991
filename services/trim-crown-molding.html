Trim & Crown Molding

Elegant trim work that adds character and value to any room.

Schedule this service

Overview

Add architectural interest to your home with custom trim and crown molding. Our skilled carpenters install baseboards, chair rails, wainscoting, and crown molding with precision. We match existing styles or create new profiles to enhance your interior design.

What's included

  • Professional installation with seamless joints
  • Wide variety of profiles and wood species
  • Can match historic or modern styles
  • Increases home value and aesthetic appeal

How it works

1

Measurement & Material Selection

We measure your rooms and help you choose molding profiles and wood types that fit your budget and style.

2

Custom Milling

We mill the molding to your exact specifications, ensuring perfect fit and finish.

3

Installation & Touch-Up

Our team installs the trim with precision, fills nail holes, and applies caulk for a polished look.
