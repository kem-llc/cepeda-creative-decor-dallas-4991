Custom Furniture

Handcrafted tables, chairs, beds, and more—designed to your exact specifications.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our custom furniture service brings your vision to life. From elegant dining tables to functional bookshelves, we use traditional joinery and modern techniques to create pieces that are both beautiful and durable. Every project starts with a consultation to understand your style, space, and needs.

What's included

  • Unique designs that match your decor
  • High-quality solid wood construction
  • Built to last with heirloom-quality craftsmanship
  • Eco-friendly material options available

How it works

1

Consultation

We meet with you to discuss your ideas, measure your space, and recommend materials and designs.

2

Design & Approval

We create detailed drawings and a 3D model for your approval before any wood is cut.

3

Build & Delivery

Our craftsmen build your piece in our workshop, then deliver and install it in your home.

Common questions

How long does it take to build a custom piece of furniture?

Timelines vary by complexity, but most pieces are completed within 2-4 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during the consultation.

What types of wood do you use?

We work with a variety of hardwoods including oak, walnut, maple, cherry, and more. We also offer reclaimed and eco-friendly options.
