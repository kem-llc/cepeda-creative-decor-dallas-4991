Our story

Crafting Quality Woodwork in Dallas Since 2008

To create beautiful, functional woodwork that enhances homes and lives through exceptional craftsmanship and personalized service.

Cepeda Creative Decor was founded by master carpenter Juan Cepeda, who brought his passion for woodworking from his family's workshop in Mexico to the heart of Dallas. For over 15 years, we have been serving homeowners and businesses across the metroplex, building a reputation for precision, creativity, and reliability.

Every project we undertake is a collaboration. We listen to your ideas, offer expert guidance, and pour our skill into every cut and finish. Whether it's a custom dining table, a kitchen renovation, or a backyard pergola, we treat your home as if it were our own.
