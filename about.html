Our story

Dallas's Premier Custom Carpentry Team

Our mission is to transform spaces through exceptional woodwork, combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design to create pieces that enrich our clients' lives.

Cepeda Creative Decor was founded in 2010 by master carpenter Juan Cepeda, who started his career building furniture in his garage. With a passion for woodworking and an eye for detail, Juan quickly built a reputation for quality and reliability. Over the years, he assembled a team of skilled artisans who share his commitment to excellence.

Today, we serve homeowners and businesses across the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex. Every project, from a simple shelf to a complete kitchen renovation, receives the same dedication and care. We believe in building relationships as strong as the furniture we create, and we're proud to be a trusted name in Dallas carpentry.
